Welcome to Al-Amal

We are a non-profit organization based in Abuja, Nigeria.

WHO WE ARE

We Work to Deliver Essentials and Give Hope to the Poor, Needy and Marginalized. We believe that Education remains the most important and valuable asset to our Main Objective of Developing Opportunities for the targeted Rural Communities, hence our scholarship Programme for the Orphans, and the educational support project for Nomadic schools.

WHERE WE started

Against the backdrop of humanitarian crises, a diverse group of professionals and community leaders came together to discuss how they could leverage their collective assets and energy to improve the living conditions of these needy individuals especially the rural and nomadic populous . From these Conversations Came the need for AL- AMAL INITIATIVE , Since then, AL-MAL INITIATIVE (AI) has achieved an impressive record of success while serving some of the most vulnerable populations including refugees, internally displaced persons (IDPs) and residents of rural areas by offering Scholarships to pupils of primary schools as well as advocacy for girl child education ,vocational training for rural women.etc . . In seeking to nurture these communities, the organization has focused on providing both immediate crisis reliefs in the form of food, clothes and medical carnivals, as well as long term development in the form of education, self-sufficiency projects, and access to potable water. To allow AL-AMAL INITIATIVE (AI) to better support and understand the communities it serves, the organization opened a network of field Volunteers across the rural area and has cultivated partnerships with local community leaders ,the youth , women leaders as well as religious leaders being important agents of change.

Aims and Objectives

The aims and objectives of the organization are:

  • To help Orphans ,Children of Special Needs and the Less privileged of the forgotten rural populous .
  • To provide proper health care to the less privileged children
  • To promote the welfare of the underprivileged ,the orphans and those in need
  • To facilitate access to education for the under privileged, the orphans and those in need .
  • To promote the welfare of the underprivileged by providing those things that are considered the basic necessities of life-food, clothing, improvement on accommodation and shelter
  • To build the minds of young persons and sensitize them for a better society with diverse culture, religious tolerance and understanding of their differences.

MISSION

To strive to alleviate the suffering from hunger, illiteracy, diseases, and poverty in the Rural areas wherever possible to reach.

VISION

Many of us take for granted the blessings we have in our lives such as food, clean drinking water, clothing, shelter and good health..etc. While so many people throughout the world can barely feed themselves and their families. Based on Islam, the best deeds for a Muslim, are those which continue to benefit Other People far beyond his or her years spent in this World.

Therefore our vision in Al-Amal Initiatives includes the following:

  • Dignity is more important to the human spirit than wealth
  • Neither grants nor markets alone will solve the problems of poverty

The Amal Initiative (AI) Difference

AL-AMAL INITIATIVE (AI) ‘s presence not just to serve communities in need, but rather to impact its supporters’ and founders’ own communities in need. Staff of the organization speak the languages of the Localities, understand the cultural context, know the challenges at stake, and are passionate about empowering and sustaining their brothers and sisters. The organization has talented and dedicated teams who go out into the field to forge the relationships with communities .This is the main reason behind the Success of AL-AMAL INITIATIVE (AI)’s programs in addressing the Basic Needs Of its target Communities.
